The objective of this project is to help X Education improve its lead conversion rate by identifying and prioritizing the most promising prospects, known as Hot Leads. The goal is to reduce unnecessary outreach efforts and enable the team to focus communication on leads with the highest likelihood of enrollment.

This project aims to develop a logistic regression model to assign lead scores (0 to 100) for effective lead targeting, empowering the company to prioritize and pursue high-potential leads for increased conversion rates.
